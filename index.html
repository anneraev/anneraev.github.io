InnerVention

A React.js app that helps the user keep track of their personal challenges. Often times, we know what we need to do to navigate those challenges, but are unable to summon up that wisdom in the moment. Connecting numerous game design communities and resources under one search bar!

PluginSleuth

Built with .Net Core MVC, Entity and Identy Frameworks, this app was made to alleviate a frustration of mine as a member of a community of people who work with smaller game design engines (less popular than, say, the Unity engine). Finding resources such as plugins and libraries for these engines can be an arduous journey through irrelevant google results and massive forum threads. PluginSleuth aims to provide a directory for all these resources, linking them together under one comprehensive search and versioning system.

This was my back-end capstone for Nashville Software School
