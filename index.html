About

Full Stack Web developer with a creative background.

I'm a self-actualized coder and problem solver with a life long love of learning. I started teaching myself coding while persuing a career as a stage actor. Eventually, I decided I wanted to persue development as a career. While I still have an interest in game development and live theatre, I am devoting myself to the development of web applications that help people and change lives. The same desire that once drove me into the performing arts now drives me as a developer: the desire for a people-oriented career in which there's always something new to learn and new problems to solve.
